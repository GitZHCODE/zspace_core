Detailed Description

Collection of methods for force density method.

Collection of vault utility methods.

Collection of vault get attribute methods.

Collection of vault set attribute methods.

Collection of methods for thrust netwrok anaysis.

Based on Schek, H-J. "The force density method for form finding and computation of general networks." Computer methods in applied mechanics and engineering 3.1 (1974): 115-134. (https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/0045782574900450) and Linkwitz, K. (2014). Force density method. Shell Structures for Architecture: Form Finding and Optimization, Routledge, Oxon and New York, 59-71.

Based on Block, Philippe, and John Ochsendorf. "Thrust network analysis: A new methodology for three-dimensional equilibrium." Journal of the International Association for shell and spatial structures 48.3 (2007): 167-173.
